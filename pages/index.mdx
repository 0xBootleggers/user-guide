# DAOhaus User Guide

Hello there, and welcome to the world of [DAOhaus](https://daohaus.club/)! Whether you're a seasoned DAO enthusiast or just beginning your journey into decentralized organizations, we're thrilled to have you here.

The DAOhaus User Guide provides a basic overview of features available in the [Summoner](https://summon.daohaus.club/) and [Admin](https://admin.daohaus.club/) apps. These apps were built by the PublicHAUS community to interface with the Moloch v3 contracts. Utlimately, we hope they help you coordinate around a purpose and inspire you to continue building on this foundation.

This guide is designed with everyone in mind. It's your go-to resource to summon and operate a Moloch DAO, providing detailed instructions and insights to help you at every step. We've broken the guide down into key sections making it easy for you to find what you need:

1. **[Summoner](summoner)**: Looking to create a DAO? Learn how to deploy your very own DAO contracts using the DAOhaus [Summoner App](https://summon.daohaus.club/). We walk you through the process from start to finish.
2. **[Admin](admin)**: Contributing to an existing DAO? Explore how to navigate and operate your DAOs more effectively with the DAOhaus [Admin App](https://admin.daohaus.club/). We describe the features available in the interface.
3. **[Glossary](glossary)**: New to the world of DAOs and feeling overwhelmed with the devilish jargon? Don't worry, we've got you covered! Our glossary explains key terminology in clear, simple language.

We're excited for you to join the community and start exploring the fascinating world of Moloch DAOs. Happy summoning and operating!
